About Almaty

Nestled at the foot of the Tian Shan mountains, Almaty is Kazakhstan's vibrant cultural heart. It's a green, spacious city famous for its apple orchards, Soviet-era landmarks, and modern energy. You can explore lively markets, visit the iconic Zenkov Cathedral, and ski at world-class resortsâ€”all within sight of snow-capped peaks. Almaty seamlessly blends stunning nature with urban life.

Why you should visit Almaty?

  • Stunning natural backdrop of the majestic Tian Shan mountains, providing a dramatic view from almost anywhere in the city.
  • Perfect blend of adventure and culture, where you can go skiing at a world-class resort in the morning and explore historic sites and museums in the afternoon.
  • Unique green metropolis, filled with sprawling parks, tree-lined avenues, and famous apple orchards, offering a refreshing urban atmosphere.
  • Vibrant and authentic local experiences at bustling markets like the Green Bazaar, where you can taste traditional foods and feel the city's pulse.
  • Fascinating architectural mix, from the colorful, wooden Zenkov Cathedral to Soviet-era landmarks and modern skyscrapers.
  • Gateway to incredible day trips, serving as the perfect base for exploring the stunning landscapes of the wider Almaty Region, like Big Almaty Lake and Charyn Canyon.
