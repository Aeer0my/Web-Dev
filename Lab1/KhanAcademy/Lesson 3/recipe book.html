Recipe #1 Avocado Tuna Salad Sandwich

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
can no-salt-added, water-packed tuna, drained and flaked 1 (5-ounce)
cup reduced-fat strained (Greek-style) yogurt 1/4
tablespoons finely chopped celery (1 small stalk) 3
tablespoons mayonnaise 2
tablespoons chopped dill pickles 2
tablespoons chopped red onion 2
tablespoon chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley 1
teaspoons Dijon mustard 2
teaspoons lemon juice 2
teaspoon crushed red pepper 1/4
teaspoon salt 1/8
slices whole-wheat bread, toasted 4 (1-ounce)
medium avocado, thinly sliced 1/2
large leaves butter lettuce 2
large slices tomato (¼-inch thick) 4

Step 1:
Stir flaked tuna, ¼ cup yogurt, 3 tablespoons celery, 2 tablespoons each mayonnaise, pickles and onion, 1 tablespoon parsley, 2 teaspoons each mustard and lemon juice, ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper and ⅛ teaspoon salt together in a small bowl until well combined.

Step 2:
Divide the tuna mixture between 2 toast slices. Top with avocado slices, lettuce, tomato slices and the remaining 2 toast slices. Cut into halves or quarters.

Recipe #2 Peanut Butter & Berries Waffle Sandwich

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 minute
Ingredients Quantity
whole-wheat freezer waffle, toasted 1
tablespoon crunchy natural peanut butter 1
cup fresh mixed berries, such as raspberries, strawberries and blackberries 1/4
teaspoon honey 1/2

Step 1:
Slice waffle in half down the center. Spread peanut butter over one half.

Step 2:
Place berries in a small bowl and smash with the back of a fork. Spread on top of the peanut butter layer and then drizzle with honey. Top with the remaining waffle half.

Recipe #3 Cucumber & Tomato Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 minute
Ingredients Quantity
cream cheese, at room temperature 2 ounces
tablespoon low-fat plain strained (Greek-style) yogurt 1
tablespoon chopped fresh chives 1
tablespoon finely chopped fresh dill 1
teaspoon ground pepper plus 1/8 teaspoon, divided 1/4
slices whole-wheat sandwich bread 2
cup thinly sliced English cucumber 1/3
small tomato, cored and cut into 1/4-inch slices 1
teaspoon salt 1/8

Step 1:
Stir cream cheese, yogurt, chives, dill and 1/4 teaspoon pepper together in a small bowl until well blended. Spread the mixture evenly on 1 side of each bread slice. Arrange cucumber slices and tomato slices in an even layer over the cream cheese mixture on 1 bread slice; sprinkle with salt and the remaining 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Top with the remaining bread slice, cream cheese-side down. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally.
